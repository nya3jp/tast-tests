RPS_F_docomo_2: (RPS_F_docomo_2.hevc)
Frame rate: 30 fps
Picture size: 832x480
Spec version: HM11.0

(Category: RPS; Sub-category: Slice header RPS with short-term pictures and inter-RPS prediction)

The purpose of the stream is to exercise the inter-RPS prediction method for sending the RPS for short-term pictures in the slice header. The inter-RPS prediction signals some RPS entries that are not used by the current picture. (used_by_curr_pic_flag[ j ] equal to 0 and use_delta_flag[ j ] equal to 1).
